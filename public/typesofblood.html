Types of Blood

O-

A blood donation occurs when a person voluntarily has drawn the blood and used for transfusion and/or made into biopharmaceutical medications by a process called fractionation (separation of whole-blood components). Donation may be of whole blood, or of specific components directly (the latter called apheresis). Blood banks often participate in the collection process as well as the procedures that follow it.
Everyone can receive O negative red blood cells
